OUR JOURNEY

FROM A SMALL DREAM TO A FITNESS FAMILY

Every milestone reflects our commitment to inspiring healthier, stronger, and happier lives. From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted fitness destination, our journey continues with our incredible members.

2018

The Beginning

YVR Gym started with a vision to create a premium fitness environment where everyone could achieve their dream body. With just a few machines and passionate trainers, we welcomed our very first members.

2019

Growing Community

Within a year, our family expanded rapidly. Hundreds of members trusted us to guide their fitness transformation.

2020

New Equipment

We upgraded our facility with imported commercial-grade equipment, offering world-class strength and cardio training.

2021

Certified Trainers
